# AI Birthday Surprise Generator 🎂✨

A premium, fully dynamic birthday surprise platform. Enter details about your special someone, select a vibe, and watch as the AI-powered engine generates a personalized interactive story, themes, and heartfelt messages.

## 🚀 Key Features
- **AI-Powered Story Engine**: Automatically generates a unique 5-10 page surprise flow based on relationship and personality.
- **Vibe Themes**: 5 distinct visual styles (Cute, Romantic, Funny, Elegant, Anime).
- **Dynamic Storytelling**: Smart text generation that adapts to nicknames, genders, and descriptions.
- **Creator Dashboard**: A modern, glassmorphic panel to customize every detail.
- **Shareable Links**: Encodes the entire surprise into a single link—no database required!

## 🎨 Themes
- **Cute**: Pastel pinks & soft vibes.
- **Romantic**: Deep reds & heart glows.
- **Funny**: Playful yellows & colorful energy.
- **Elegant**: Dark aesthetic & gold accents.
- **Anime**: Kawaii Japanese style.

## 🛠️ How to Use
1. Open `creator.html` in your browser.
2. Fill in the recipient's details (Name, Vibe, Relationship, etc.).
3. Click **Generate Magic ✨**.
4. Share the generated link with your loved one!

## 📁 Project Structure
- `index.html`: The interactive viewer.
- `creator.html`: The AI dashboard.
- `css/style.css`: Theme-aware design system.
- `js/script.js`: Surprise playback engine.
- `js/creator.js`: AI generation logic.
